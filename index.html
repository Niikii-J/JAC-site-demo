About The Company


Mission:
The primary focus of James A. Carey & Associates is to provide superior security services for sports, musical and cultural events, as well as institutions in The Bahamas.


Vision:
Our vision is to be The Bahamas’s stop security company, meet the continuously evolving security needs for events and business in The Bahamas.


Values:
James A. Carey & Associates prides itself on offering reliable and professional services. Our personnel adhere to the highest standards of ethical and moral conduct, while acting in the best interest of our clients.



Our History



  • Humble Beginnings

    About the Founder

    Our president, James A. Carey, was the first direct enlistee to The Royal Bahamas Police Force. He retired in 2009 as an Assistant Commissioner after 32 years of dedicated service to that organization. After retirement, he volunteered as a security consultant for various organizations. To date, he has over 40 years of Law Enforcement, forensic science, life and property protection experience.

  • Director of Security

    CARIFTA Games in 2013

    In 2013, he was asked to serve as Director of Security for the 2013 CARIFTA Games that were held in The Bahamas at the newly constructed Thomas A. Robinson National Stadium. As this was the first major event to be held at this 15,000 seat arena he had to develop the security plans and trained all personnel in security and emergency procedures for this venue.

  • Sports and Entertainment

    Official Company Formation

    Following these games, he recognized a void in the emerging sports and entertainment industry. In an effort to fill that need, James A. Carey & Associates Security Consultants was formed and our president used this as an opportunity to hire many of the unemployed persons who served as volunteer security officers during the 2013 CARIFTA Games.

  • Security Matrix and Emergency

    Procedures in 2017

    In 2017 James A. Carey & Associates was contracted to provide security services for the construction of the beach soccer at the foot of Sidney Poitier Bridge (Paradise Island). Later that same year, we were asked to develop the security Matrix and emergency procedures & evacuation plans for that site and provide security services for the first soccer matches that were held at 2,000 seat stadium.

Officer Training

During some events, James A. Carey & Associates have used upwards of 200 officer per event, and many of these highly trained officers are available for immediate deployment. Included among these officers are former law enforcement personnel from the Royal Bahamas Police and Defense Forces and The Department of corrections.

In addition to our standard training ...

in the areas listed above, we have developed both a basic and an advanced training courses that we offer to our officers and have conducted for various organizations throughout The Bahamas. Our security supervisors are required to undergo and additional orientation training prior to each posting. All our officers are licensed with the Ministry of National Security as Inquiry Agents and Security Guards. They are vatted annually by the Royal Bahamas Police Force.

Additionally, all our personnel ...

are required to undergo random drug & alcohol testing. Our customer service programs are continually updated in response to industry needs. While on duty, all security officers of James A Carey & Associates are required to follow the code of ethics developed by the private Security Advisory Counsel and be dressed in our uniform of white security shirt with black trousers and a burgundy tie during winter months.

They will have security patches ...

on both sleeves, additionally officers would wear identification badges. All security officers will also be equipped with island wide hand-held radio which will permit them to remain in constant communication with supervisors and our control room. All officer & employees are requested to keep all information obtained from our clients in strict confidentiality. Any beach of this will result in immediate termination.

Mobile Patrol

James A. Carey & Associates provide supervisors who will patrol and spot check all locations during each shift. We will also provide any special patrol our clients may request.
